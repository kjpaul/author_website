## COOKIE POLICY

**Our website uses cookie technology to offer our users a more complete browsing experience.**

OrbitalRing.Space adopts cookie technology to make navigation simple and efficient for our users. Users who browse and interact with our website will see a minimum amount of information entered on the devices in use, i.e. computers and mobile devices, in small text files called “cookies”, saved in the directories used by the user’s browser.

**Disabling cookies** is an open option for all users, but without cookies some of the services on the website may not work properly and some pages will not be displayed correctly.

**TYPES OF COOKIES**

There are various types of cookies, some to make site navigation easier and more effective, others to enable certain features. The cookies used on our website allow to:

• memorize the preferences entered by users during the interaction with the site;
• facilitate the sharing of site content on social networks by users;
• analyze the use of the services and content provided by the site to optimize the browsing experience and services offered to the user.

**TECHNICAL COOKIES**

This type of cookie is strictly necessary for the proper functioning of some sections of the website. There are two categories of cookies: persistent cookies and session cookies.

**Persistent cookies:** once the browser is closed they are not deleted but remain active until the preset expiration date.

**Session cookies:** are deleted every time the browser is closed. These cookies, always sent from our domain, are necessary to allow the user to view and navigate the site correctly and will always be used and sent, unless users change the settings in their browser (thus interrupting the display of pages of the site).

**THIRD PARTY ANALYSIS COOKIES**

These cookies are used in order to collect information on the use of the site by users anonymously, and in particular: pages visited, time spent, traffic origin, geographical origin, age, gender and interests for the purpose of advertising campaigns. marketing. These cookies are sent from third-party domains external to the site, in our case from the Google Analytics platform.

**COOKIES FOR THE INTEGRATION OF THIRD PARTY PRODUCTS AND SOFTWARE**

This type of cookie integrates features developed by third parties and used within the pages of the webite, such as the characters used for texts, icons, preferences expressed in social networks to share site content or for the use of software services of third parties, such as social media content republished on this website. These cookies are sent from third party domains that offer their functionality to help us create better interaction performance for the pages of the site.

**PROFILING COOKIES**

These cookies are necessary to create user profiles in order to send advertising messages in line with the preferences expressed by the user within the pages of the site. **Our website does not use profiling cookies**.

**CONSENT FOR TECHNICAL COOKIES**

According to current legislation, OrbitalRing.Space is not required to seek consent for technical cookies, as they are necessary to provide the requested services. For all other types of cookies, consent can be expressed by the user with one or more of the following methods:

• through specific browser configurations or related computer programs used to navigate the pages of the website;
• changing the settings in the use of third-party services.

Both of these solutions could prevent the user from using or viewing parts of the site or from viewing and navigating it correctly.
